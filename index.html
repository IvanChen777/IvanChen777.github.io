About Me

Hello! My name is Chen Yifeng, I have completed my MSc in Civil Engineering in EPFL, and obtained a minor in data science. My master's thesis is completed in EPFL, under the supervision of Prof. Nikolas Geroliminis. My research interests span across the artificial intelligence, computer vision, drone-related problem, intelligent transportation system, rock image analysis and so on. In EPFL, I worked as a research assistance under Prof. Alexandre Alahi and Dr. Pascal Turberg's supervision. In bachelor, I finished my study in Tongji University, major in civil engineering, and minor in math, my bachelor's thesis under Prof. Hongwei Huang's supervision and worked as a research assistant under Prof. Danhui Dan's supervision.

Except research, I am also delicated to apply the knowledge I learnt to solve the real world problem. I worked as a junior computer vision engineer in MobiLysis under Dr. Taylor Mordan's supervision, focusing on improving the performance of the tracking algorithm based on drone's video. I also had a great opportunity to work in Hellobike company as a computer vision engineer intern, focusing on the object detection, image segmentation and AI standard alignment.

Outside the acadamia and industrial work, I am interested in multiple areas, such as photography, video games, reading, cooking, singing, and traveling:)
